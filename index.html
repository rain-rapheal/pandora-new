our corporate profile

Pandora Limited is an indigenous pharmaceutical company in Nigeria, based in Lagos and incorporated on the 25th March 1999 with RC No.353658. The operational philosophy of Pandora Limited is to bring quality healthcare to the door step of all Nigerians

We deliver quality natural care through the use of natural products all manufactured in the United States of America under the brand name “NATURE’S FIELD” that offers high quality natural supplements formulated by a team of experts including doctors, pharmacists, nutritionists, scientists and laboratory technicians in the United States of America. We

Our products are manufactured in in line with the strictest pharmaceutical Good Manufacturing Practice (GMP) at a production facility certified by the US National Foods Association (NNFA) and the Food and Drug Association (FDA). We offer specific natural supplements uniquely formulated to provide you with maximum nutritional benefits. This is the result of our efforts to blend nature with science. All our products are fully registered with the National Agency for Food and Drug Administration and Control (NAFDAC).
